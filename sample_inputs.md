# 🧪 Sample Test Inputs for Clarity AI

Use these to demo or test the app. Paste any of them into the text box and hit **Analyze**.

---

## 1. Career Decision

> I'm a mid-level software engineer with 5 years of experience. I've received a job offer from a startup that pays 20% more but has no job security. My current company is stable but growth is slow. I also have an idea for my own SaaS product. What should I do?

---

## 2. Financial Decision

> I have $30,000 in savings. I can either invest in an index fund, put a down payment on a rental property, or pay off my student loans which have a 5.5% interest rate. I'm 28 and single with no dependents.

---

## 3. Personal / Relocation

> I've been offered a chance to move abroad for 2 years for work. My partner has a stable job here and we just adopted a dog. The opportunity would double my salary and boost my career, but I'm worried about the relationship and settling in a new country.

---

## 4. Education

> I'm 25 and working as a data analyst. I got accepted into a top-20 MBA program but it costs $120,000 and I'd have to quit my job. Alternatively, I could do a part-time online master's in data science for $15,000 while keeping my income. My goal is to move into product management.

---

## 5. Startup vs Stability

> My friend and I built a side project that's getting 500 daily active users with zero marketing. We both have full-time jobs paying $110k each. We're debating whether to go full-time on the startup, keep it as a side project, or try to raise a small seed round while staying employed.

---

## 6. Health & Lifestyle

> I'm a freelance designer working 60-hour weeks. The money is great but I'm burning out, gaining weight, and my sleep is terrible. I could hire a subcontractor and take a pay cut, reduce my client load, or switch to a salaried in-house role with benefits and work-life balance.
